Star Force Genesis

Star Force Genesis is my 6th and last Semester Project during my course of studies in Animation & Game. Together with 4 other Animation & Game students and 3 Sound & Music Production students we created this retroesque side scrolling space shooter.
Since we were 5 main members instead of the usual 4, we split the programming workflow up: While the second programmer created a rough prototype, I worked on different game systems, tools and DOTS integrations.
